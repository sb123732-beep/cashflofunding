FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about our funding products and process.

A merchant cash advance (MCA) is a form of business financing where you receive a lump sum of capital in exchange for a percentage of your future daily credit card sales or bank deposits. Unlike a traditional loan, repayment fluctuates with your revenue — making it ideal for businesses with variable or seasonal income.
Most applications receive a decision within 2–4 hours of submission. Once approved and contracts are signed, funds are typically deposited into your business account the same day or within 24–48 hours. The entire process often happens within one business day.
We focus primarily on your business's revenue, cash flow, and time in operation. Our underwriting team evaluates your actual business performance to determine the right funding solution and amount for your situation.
Funding amounts range from $5,000 to $5,000,000 or more, depending on your average monthly revenue, time in business, and the product selected. Typically, you can qualify for 50%–150% of your average monthly revenue. Speak with a funding advisor for a personalized estimate.
Prepayment terms vary by product. Some funding products allow early payoff with potential discounts on remaining fees. Your advisor will clearly outline all prepayment terms before you sign any agreement. We believe in full transparency.
We require a minimum of 6 months in business and at least $10,000 in average monthly revenue. If your business is under 6 months old, we recommend applying once you've hit that milestone. For businesses approaching these thresholds, reach out to an advisor — sometimes exceptions can be made case-by-case.
